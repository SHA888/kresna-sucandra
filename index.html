About

Rooted in Bali,
building for the world.

I am an anesthesiologist and intensive therapy consultant with 13 years of clinical experience, currently pursuing a doctoral degree focused on applying machine learning to early sepsis detection — a problem that kills millions annually and where earlier prediction saves lives.

Alongside clinical practice, I build technology: from low-level Rust crates and compiler tooling to quantum-AI hybrids and clinical decision systems. I believe the most important breakthroughs happen at the intersection of domains that rarely talk to each other.

I am the CTO of CiptaSel, a Bali-based holding company working across precision medicine, Indonesian biodiversity commercialisation, and green infrastructure — attempting to prove that deep tech can grow from a small island.

Outside medicine and code, I am a musician, a student of Balinese traditional healing (Usada), and a steward of Taru Pramana — ancient manuscripts encoding millennia of botanical knowledge. I write on Medium and Substack.
